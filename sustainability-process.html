PROCESS

Recycled plastic turned into quality bags.

Follow the journey of our bags from plastic
waste to factory..

  • 01. Collect

    • Our materials come from several sources, some of which include ocean cleanups and excess plastics from other productions. These are collected by our suppliers and brought to a facility for processing.

  • 02. Sort

    • After the plastic has been collected, it is cleaned and sorted at a facility that specialises in creating rPET . rPET stands for recycled polyethylene terephthalate, or recycled PET. It can for example be made into blankets, insulation, shoes and other clothing items.

  • 03. Rip

    • Once the collected plastic has been sorted, it is sent to a shredder where it is ripped into smaller, more flexible pieces that are easier to work with. These pieces are then cleaned once more, before being taken to another facility where they are processed and turned into the material we make our bags with.

    Learn more about our materials
  • 04. Production

    • When the rPET material has been finished, it is taken to our production facilities where it is used for shaping and sewing our bags. Here our workers cut the fabric and sew it into the bags you see on our website. Handles, zippers and other details are attached, and quality checks are performed before the bags are sent to our warehouse.

    Learn more about our factories
  • 05. Shipping
